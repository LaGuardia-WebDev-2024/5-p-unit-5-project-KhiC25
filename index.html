Brooklyn United's Turkey Day

What Is Turkey Day?

Turkey Day is one of Brooklyn's BIGGEST Marching Band, Drumline and Dance events in November. This year Turkey Day will take place from November 30th - December 1st at Uncommoon Charter School. Why should you go to Turkey day? (other than fellow LaGuardians, like myself being part of the show). But because, it is a great time waiting to be had by special Dancelines and Drumlines from across all the big cities.
